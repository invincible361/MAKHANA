Discover the ancient wisdom of Makhana, a traditional Indian superfood

What is Makhana?

Makhana, also known as Fox Nuts or Lotus Seeds, is a traditional Indian superfood derived from the seeds of the Euryale Ferox plant, predominantly grown in the wetlands of Bihar, India, especially the Mithilanchal region.

Nutritional Value & Health Benefits

Makhana is low in calories, high in protein, gluten-free, and rich in essential nutrients like calcium, potassium, magnesium, phosphorus, and antioxidants.

What is GI-Tag Storytelling in Branding?

It's when you highlight the regional legacy and traditional craft of your product in your packaging, marketing, and story.

"Sourced from the ponds of Mithila, hand-harvested using centuries-old traditions."

"Mithila Makhana – a GI-tagged superfood, cherished since ancient times."
